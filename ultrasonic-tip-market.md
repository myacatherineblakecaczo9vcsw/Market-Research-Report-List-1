<p><p>Ultrasonic tip market consists of two main types: flexible and normal tips. Flexible tips are designed with a flexible material, allowing for easier access to hard-to-reach areas and the ability to adapt to irregular surfaces. They are commonly used in applications where maneuverability is crucial, such as medical procedures or industrial inspections. On the other hand, normal tips are made of a rigid material and are typically used in conventional ultrasonic testing, providing stable and accurate results. Each type offers specific advantages depending on the intended use and requirements of the market.</p></p>
