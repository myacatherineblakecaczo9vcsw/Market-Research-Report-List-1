<p><p>PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) software is available in two main market types: on-cloud and on-premise. On-cloud PLM software is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the internet. It offers the advantage of easy accessibility, scalability, and reduced hardware costs. On-premise PLM software is installed and maintained on the client's local servers and accessed through their internal network. It provides complete control over data and customization but requires higher infrastructure investment and maintenance. Both options have their own benefits, and the choice depends on the organization's specific needs and priorities.</p></p>
