<p><ul><li>Cold Chamber</li><li>Hot Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Pressure Die Casting Machine market can be divided into two main types: Cold Chamber and Hot Chamber machines. Cold Chamber machines are used for casting high melting point metals, such as aluminum and copper alloys, as the metal is fed into the chamber from an external melting furnace. Hot Chamber machines, on the other hand, are used for casting low melting point metals, such as zinc and magnesium, as the metal is melted directly within the chamber using a piston. Both types of machines play a critical role in the production of various industrial components.</p></p>
