<p><p>Bullet resistant auto glass is available in several market types, including polycarbonate, acrylic, glass-clad polycarbonate, and poly-vinyl butyral (PVB). Polycarbonate auto glass offers high impact resistance and durability, while acrylic auto glass is lightweight and cost-effective. Glass-clad polycarbonate combines the strength of polycarbonate with the clarity of glass, making it a popular choice for luxury vehicles. Poly-vinyl butyral (PVB) auto glass is made of layers of glass and PVB, providing both strength and visibility. Each type of bullet resistant auto glass caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
