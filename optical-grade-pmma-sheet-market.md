<p><strong>The Optical-grade PMMA Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast PMMA Sheet</li><li>Extruded PMMA Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical-grade PMMA sheets are available in two main types: cast PMMA sheet and extruded PMMA sheet. Cast PMMA sheets are produced by casting liquid PMMA into molds, resulting in a high level of optical clarity and surface quality. Extruded PMMA sheets are created by pushing heated PMMA through a die, offering cost-effective and easy-to-process options. Both types are widely used in applications requiring exceptional optical properties, such as lighting fixtures, displays, and architectural glazing.</p></p>
