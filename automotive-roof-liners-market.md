<p><p>Automotive roof liners are available in two main types: thermoplastics roof liners and thermoset roof liners. Thermoplastics roof liners are made from materials that can be melted and reshaped, making them flexible and easy to mold. Thermoset roof liners, on the other hand, are made from materials that undergo a chemical change when heated, becoming rigid and durable. Both types of roof liners serve the same purpose of providing insulation, soundproofing, and aesthetic enhancement to the interior of vehicles.</p></p>
