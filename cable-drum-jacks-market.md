<p><p>Cable drum jacks are used to lift and transport heavy cable drums in various industrial applications. There are three main types of cable drum jacks available in the market - hydraulic, mechanical, and manual. Hydraulic cable drum jacks use fluid power to lift and lower the drums, providing smooth and efficient operation. Mechanical jacks utilize gears and other mechanical components for lifting purposes. Manual jacks require physical effort to lift the drums, making them more suitable for smaller cable drum sizes or light-duty applications.</p></p>
