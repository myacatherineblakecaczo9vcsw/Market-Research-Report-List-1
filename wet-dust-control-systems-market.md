<p><p>Wet dust control systems are used to control and reduce the amount of dust particles in industrial processes. Two common types of wet dust control systems include wet scrubbers and Wet Electrostatic Precipitators (WEPS). Wet scrubbers use water to trap and remove dust particles from the air, while WEPS use an electric charge to capture and remove the particles. These systems are effective in reducing air pollution and improving air quality in various industries such as manufacturing, mining, and agriculture.</p></p>
