<p><p>Sodium lamps are commonly used in outdoor lighting and come in two main types: High Pressure Sodium (HPS) lamps and Low Pressure Sodium (LPS) lamps. HPS lamps emit a bright white light and are more energy efficient, making them ideal for street lighting and industrial applications. On the other hand, LPS lamps emit a monochromatic yellow light and are extremely efficient, making them suitable for lighting highways and other areas where color rendering is not important. Both types of lamps have their own unique advantages and are widely utilized in different outdoor lighting scenarios.</p></p>
