<p><p>Electric traction wire ropes are classified into two main types based on the core material: fiber core and steel core. Fiber core wire ropes are made with synthetic fibers like polypropylene or sisal which provide flexibility and light weight. Steel core wire ropes, on the other hand, are reinforced with steel strands which enhance strength and durability. Both types have their advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements for the traction system.</p></p>
