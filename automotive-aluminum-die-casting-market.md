<p><strong>The Automotive Aluminum Die Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Type</li><li>Cold Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive aluminum die casting market is divided into hot chamber type and cold chamber type. Hot chamber die casting involves melting the aluminum within the casting machine itself, making it suitable for high-volume production of smaller parts. Cold chamber die casting, on the other hand, involves melting the aluminum in a separate furnace before transferring it to the casting machine, allowing for the production of larger and more complex parts. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in the automotive industry for manufacturing various components.</p></p>
