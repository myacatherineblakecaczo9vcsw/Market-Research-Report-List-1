<p><p>Outdoor Packaged Substation includes Liquid-Filled Transformers and Dry Type Transformers. Liquid-Filled Transformers are immersed in oil or other liquid insulation, providing efficient cooling and electrical insulation. They are commonly used for medium to high voltage applications. Dry Type Transformers use solid insulation materials such as resin or epoxy, eliminating the risk of flammable liquids. They are preferred for indoor locations where safety is a concern. Both types offer different advantages and are suitable for different environments based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
