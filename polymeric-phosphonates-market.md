<p><p>Polymeric phosphonates market includes various types such as HEDP, ATMP, PBTC, DTPMP, BHMTPMPA, and HPAA. HEDP or hydroxyethylidene diphosphonic acid is widely used in water treatment processes. ATMP or amino tris (methylenephosphonic acid) is used as a chelating agent. PBTC or 2-phosphonobutane -1,2,4-tricarboxylic acid is utilized in industrial water treatment. DTPMP or diethylene triamine penta methylene phosphonic acid is a scale inhibitor. BHMTPMPA or bis (hexamethylene) triamine penta methylene phosphonic acid is used in corrosion inhibition. HPAA or hydroxyphosphono- acetic acid is employed in various applications such as textiles, detergents, and water treatment.</p></p>
