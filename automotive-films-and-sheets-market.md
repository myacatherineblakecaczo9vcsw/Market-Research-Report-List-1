<p><p>Automotive films and sheets market can be categorized into various types such as LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE, and others. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) films and sheets are known for their flexibility and impact resistance. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) films are widely used for applications requiring high tensile strength. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) films are preferred for their durability and resistance to chemicals. The "Others" category may include specialty films and sheets with unique properties tailored for specific automotive applications.</p></p>
