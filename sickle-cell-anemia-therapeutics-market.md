<p><ul><li>Blood Transfusion</li><li>Pharmacotherapy</li><li>Bone Marrow Transplant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sickle cell anemia therapeutics market includes blood transfusion, pharmacotherapy, and bone marrow transplant. Blood transfusion involves removing and replacing abnormal red blood cells with healthy ones. Pharmacotherapy includes drugs that help manage symptoms and prevent complications. Bone marrow transplant is a procedure where diseased bone marrow is replaced with healthy donor marrow to produce normal red blood cells. These treatments aim to alleviate symptoms, prevent complications, and improve quality of life for individuals with sickle cell anemia.</p></p>
