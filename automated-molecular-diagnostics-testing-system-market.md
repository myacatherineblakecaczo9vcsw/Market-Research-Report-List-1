<p><p>The Automated Molecular Diagnostics Testing System market includes various types of diagnostic tests. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a technique that amplifies specific DNA segments for detection of pathogens or genetic variations. FISH (Fluorescent In-situ Hybridization) helps identify chromosomal abnormalities using fluorescent-labeled probes. Spectral Karyotype Imaging enables visualization of individual chromosomes and their abnormalities. DNA Microarrays analyze thousands of genes simultaneously to identify genetic variations and assess gene expression. These technologies play a crucial role in diagnosing various diseases by quickly and accurately detecting genetic and chromosomal abnormalities.</p></p>
