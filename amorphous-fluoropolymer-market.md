<p><strong>The Amorphous Fluoropolymer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Solution</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous fluoropolymers are used in two main markets: solid and solution. In the solid market, amorphous fluoropolymers are used in the form of pellets, powders, or coatings, which are applied directly to the surface of various materials for protection against corrosion, chemical resistance, and non-stick properties. In the solution market, amorphous fluoropolymers are dissolved in solvents to create liquid coatings or inks that can be applied to surfaces by spraying or dipping for similar protective and functional purposes.</p></p>
