<p><p>Heat shields can be classified into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I heat shields are designed for applications where protection against high temperatures is the primary concern, such as in automotive exhaust systems. Type II heat shields, on the other hand, are used in environments where both thermal insulation and mechanical protection are required, such as in aerospace or industrial applications. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of various systems exposed to high temperatures.</p></p>
