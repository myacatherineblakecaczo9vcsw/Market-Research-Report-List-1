<p><p>The Electric Endoscopic Stapler market consists of three main types: Linear, Standard, and Circular. Linear staplers are used for linear tissue cutting and stapling during procedures. Standard staplers are typically used for general surgical procedures where a straight line of staples is needed. Circular staplers are specifically designed for circular, end-to-end anastomosis procedures. Each type of stapler caters to different surgical needs and provides unique benefits in terms of efficiency, precision, and safety in various medical procedures.</p></p>
