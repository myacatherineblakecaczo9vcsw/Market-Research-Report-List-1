<p><p>Non-silicone mold release agents are available in two main types: liquid release agents and aerosol release agents. Non-silicone liquid release agents are applied by brushing or spraying onto the mold surface, providing a barrier between the mold and the material being cast. Non-silicone aerosol release agents are easy to use and provide a thin, uniform coating on the mold surface. Both types of non-silicone mold release agents offer effective and efficient ways to prevent sticking and facilitate easy release of casted materials.</p></p>
