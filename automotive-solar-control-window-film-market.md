<p><p>Automotive solar control window films come in three main types: clear, dyed, and vacuum coated. Clear films are transparent and provide basic UV protection. Dyed films have a layer of dye that absorbs solar energy and reduces heat and glare. Vacuum coated films have a metalized layer that reflects and blocks heat and UV rays more effectively. Each type offers varying degrees of solar protection, allowing consumers to choose the level of heat and glare reduction that best suits their needs.</p></p>
