<p><p>Food and Beverage Plastic Packaging market can be divided into two main types: Flexible Packaging and Rigid Packaging. Rigid Packaging, on the other hand, consists of containers, bottles, and jars that are more sturdy and offer better protection for items like beverages and sauces. Both types play a crucial role in preserving the freshness and extending the shelf life of food and beverage products.</p></p>
