<p><p>The robotic surgery consumables market can be categorized into different types of equipment. Access and facilitation equipment involves tools that help in gaining access to the surgical site and positioning the robotic system. End effectors are devices attached to the robotic arms that perform specific tasks such as cutting, suturing, or cauterizing. Suturing instruments are used for sewing incisions during surgery. Other types of consumables in this market include accessories like trocars and robotic staplers. These equipment and instruments play a crucial role in performing surgical procedures using robotic technology.</p></p>
