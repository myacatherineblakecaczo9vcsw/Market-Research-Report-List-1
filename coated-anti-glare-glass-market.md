<p><p>Coated anti-glare glass is divided into two main types: chemical treatment and physical processing. Chemical treatment involves applying a special coating to the glass surface to reduce glare and reflections, while physical processing involves etching or embossing the surface to achieve the same effect. The choice between the two types depends on factors like cost, durability, and specific performance requirements.</p></p>
