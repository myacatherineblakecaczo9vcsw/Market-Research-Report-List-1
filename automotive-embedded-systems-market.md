<p><strong>The Automotive Embedded Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Hardware</li><li>Embedded Software</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive embedded systems market includes two main types: embedded hardware and embedded software. Embedded hardware refers to the physical components integrated within a vehicle, such as electronic control units (ECUs), sensors, and actuators. These components enable various functionalities like engine control, safety systems, and infotainment features. On the other hand, embedded software comprises the programs and codes running on these embedded hardware components. It controls and manages their operations, allowing the vehicle to perform tasks and functionalities. Both embedded hardware and software play crucial roles in ensuring the efficient functioning of automotive systems.</p></p>
