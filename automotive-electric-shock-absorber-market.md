<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Shock Absorber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Telescopic Shock Absorbers</li><li>Strut Type Shock Absorbers</li><li>Spring Seat Shock Absorbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric shock absorber market includes three main types: Conventional Telescopic Shock Absorbers, Strut Type Shock Absorbers, and Spring Seat Shock Absorbers. Conventional Telescopic Shock Absorbers are the most common type, consisting of a cylinder and piston filled with hydraulic fluid. Strut Type Shock Absorbers combine the shock absorber and suspension coil into one unit. Spring Seat Shock Absorbers, on the other hand, use a separate coil spring to absorb shocks. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
