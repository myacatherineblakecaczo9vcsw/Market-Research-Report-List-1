<p><p>Dual clutch transmission market is divided into two types: Wet Dual Clutch Technology and Dry Dual Clutch Technology. Wet dual clutch technology uses oil for cooling and lubrication, making it more efficient for higher torque applications. On the other hand, dry dual clutch technology uses no oil, resulting in lower maintenance costs and better fuel efficiency. Both technologies offer smooth shifting and improved performance, catering to different consumer preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
