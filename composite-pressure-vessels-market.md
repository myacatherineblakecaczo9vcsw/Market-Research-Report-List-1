<p><p>Composite pressure vessels market can be classified into three types based on their shape - cylindrical, spherical, and others. Cylindrical pressure vessels are elongated with uniform cross-section, providing high internal volume. Spherical pressure vessels have a rounded shape, offering even pressure distribution and maximum internal volume. The "Others" category includes vessels with unique shapes such as prismatic or conical. Each type caters to different industrial applications and requirements, providing a wide range of options for companies looking for durable and efficient pressure vessel solutions.</p></p>
