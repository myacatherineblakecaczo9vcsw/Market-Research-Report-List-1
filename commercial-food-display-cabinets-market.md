<p><p>Commercial food display cabinets come in two main types: refrigerated and heated. Refrigerated food display cabinets are designed to keep cold foods such as salads, desserts, and beverages at a chilled temperature to maintain freshness. On the other hand, heated food display cabinets are meant for hot foods like soups, pizzas, and pastries to be served at a warm, appetizing temperature. Both types of cabinets are essential for restaurants, bakeries, and other food establishments to showcase their products and attract customers.</p></p>
