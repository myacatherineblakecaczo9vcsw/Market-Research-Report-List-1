<p><p>Hovering technology is divided into two main market types: electromagnetic suspension and electrodynamics suspension. Electromagnetic suspension utilizes magnetic fields to generate levitation, while electrodynamics suspension relies on electrical currents interacting with magnetic fields to achieve levitation. Both technologies have applications in various industries such as transportation, aerospace, and consumer electronics. These markets are constantly evolving and expanding as new advancements are made in the field of hovering technology.</p></p>
