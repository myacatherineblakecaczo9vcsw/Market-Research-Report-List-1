<p><ul><li>Single Sided</li><li>Double Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone adhesive sheets come in two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided adhesive sheets have adhesive on only one side, making them ideal for applications where only one surface needs to be bonded. Double-sided adhesive sheets have adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding between two surfaces. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application, with single-sided sheets offering simplicity and ease of use, and double-sided sheets providing a more versatile bonding solution.</p></p>
