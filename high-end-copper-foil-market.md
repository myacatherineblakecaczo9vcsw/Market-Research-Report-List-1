<p><p>The high-end copper foil market consists of two main types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by rolling copper sheets into thin foils, which are primarily used in the electronics industry for circuit boards and flexible printed circuits. On the other hand, electrolytic copper foil is manufactured through an electroplating process, resulting in a high-quality foil used for a wide range of applications, including lithium-ion batteries, LED technologies, and aerospace industry. Both types cater to the demand for high-performance copper foil in various industries.</p></p>
