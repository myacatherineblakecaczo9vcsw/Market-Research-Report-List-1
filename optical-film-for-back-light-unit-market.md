<p><p>Optical films for back light unit market includes Reflective Film, Diffuser Film, and Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF). Reflective Film helps in increasing the efficiency of the light by reflecting it back into the display. Diffuser Film works in scattering the light uniformly across the display for better viewing angles. Brightness Enhancement Film (BEF) increases the brightness and energy efficiency of the display by redirecting light towards the viewer. These films play a crucial role in enhancing the overall performance and quality of the display technology.</p></p>
