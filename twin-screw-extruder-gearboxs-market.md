<p><ul><li>Solid Shaft</li><li>Hollow Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Twin-screw extruder gearboxes are commonly classified into two types based on their shaft design - solid shaft and hollow shaft. Solid shaft gearboxes have a single solid shaft that connects and transmits power between the gears. On the other hand, hollow shaft gearboxes have an inner bore that allows for additional components like cooling systems or sensors to be integrated. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications depending on the specific requirements of the extrusion process.</p></p>
