<p><strong>The AMOLED Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED Display)</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AMOLED Display is a type of display technology that uses an active matrix to control individual pixels, offering high resolution and faster response times. This technology is commonly used in smartphones and televisions. On the other hand, Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays use a simpler matrix that limits their resolution and response time, making them more suitable for smaller displays such as wearable devices or small electronic appliances. Both types of displays have their own unique advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
