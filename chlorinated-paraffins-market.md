<p><p>Chlorinated Paraffins (CP) are classified into different types based on their chlorine content, such as CP-42, CP-52, and CP-70. CP-42 refers to chlorinated paraffins with 40-50% chlorine content, CP-52 has 50-60% chlorine, and CP-70 has 70% chlorine. These different types cater to various applications in industries like plastics, rubber, paints, and metalworking. Apart from these three main types, there are also other variations of chlorinated paraffins available in the market to meet specific requirements of different end-users.</p></p>
