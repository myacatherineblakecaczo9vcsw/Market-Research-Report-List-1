<p><p>Microfiber Polyurethane Synthetic (Faux) Leather market types include Co-Blending Spinning, Composite Spinning, and Direct Spinning. Co-Blending Spinning involves blending microfiber polyurethane with other fibers during the spinning process to create a mixed material. Composite Spinning entails combining microfiber polyurethane with other materials after spinning to form a composite material. Direct Spinning involves directly spinning microfiber polyurethane into fibers without the need for additional blending or mixing. Each market type offers unique benefits and applications within the synthetic leather industry.</p></p>
