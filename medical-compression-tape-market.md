<p><p>The medical compression tape market can be divided into two types: dynamic and static. Dynamic compression tapes are designed to provide continuous pressure and support during movement, making them suitable for sports activities and post-injury recovery. On the other hand, static compression tapes are used for immobilization or to reduce swelling in non-mobile body parts, such as knees or ankles. Both types of tapes serve distinct purposes in the medical field, offering different levels of compression and support for various conditions.</p></p>
