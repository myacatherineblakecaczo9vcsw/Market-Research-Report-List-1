<p><p>Puncture-proof tire systems are available in two main types - bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires have plies that run diagonally across the tire, providing a smooth ride but less tread contact with the road. Radial tires have plies that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better tread contact for improved traction and fuel efficiency. Each type of tire has its advantages and disadvantages, making them suitable for different types of vehicles and driving conditions.</p></p>
