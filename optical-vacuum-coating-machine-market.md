<p><strong>The Optical Vacuum Coating Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Evaporation Equipment</li><li>Sputtering Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical vacuum coating machines are used for depositing thin films on various materials. The market is divided into two main types: Evaporation Equipment and Sputtering Equipment. Evaporation Equipment works by heating the coating material until it evaporates and then condenses onto the substrate, while Sputtering Equipment involves bombarding the coating material with high-energy ions to create a thin film. Both types of equipment are essential for producing high-quality coatings in industries such as electronics, optics, and aerospace.</p></p>
