<p><p>PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate) edgebands are available in three different market types based on their thickness. Thin edgebands are typically less than 1mm thick and are commonly used for furniture and cabinetry applications where a discreet edge is desired. Medium edgebands are usually between 1mm and 2mm thick and are often preferred for general furniture use. On the other hand, thick edgebands, ranging from 2mm to 3mm or more, provide enhanced durability and are commonly utilized in high-traffic areas or heavy-duty applications such as commercial furniture or kitchen countertops.</p></p>
