<p><p>LED Encapsulant for Lighting Module market is segmented into three main types: Silicone, Epoxy, and Polyurethane. Silicone encapsulants offer excellent thermal stability and flexibility, making them ideal for high-power LED modules. Epoxy encapsulants provide strong adhesion and mechanical protection, suitable for harsh environments. Polyurethane encapsulants offer a good balance of properties, including thermal conductivity and UV resistance. Each type of encapsulant serves a specific purpose in protecting and enhancing the performance of LED lighting modules.</p></p>
