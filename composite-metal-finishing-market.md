<p><p>Composite Metal Finishing refers to the application of different types of finishes on metal surfaces to enhance their appearance, protect them from corrosion, and improve their durability. Organic Finishing involves the use of organic coatings such as paints and varnishes. Inorganic Finishing includes processes like electroplating and anodizing.
