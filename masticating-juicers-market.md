<p><p>Masticating juicers, also known as slow juicers or cold press juicers, are specialized appliances that extract juice by slowly crushing fruits and vegetables. Unlike centrifugal juicers, which use high-speed spinning blades to extract juice, masticating juicers operate at a slower speed, maintaining lower levels of heat and friction. This helps to preserve the nutritional value of the juice by minimizing oxidation and heat damage.</p><p>The masticating juicers market has witnessed significant growth in recent years. <p><p>Masticating juicers have two main market types: vertical auger model and horizontal auger model. The vertical auger model has a long, upright design with a narrow feeding chute. It uses a single auger that slowly rotates to crush and extract juice from fruits and vegetables. On the other hand, the horizontal auger model has a wider feeding chute and utilizes a horizontal auger that works in a similar manner. Both models offer efficient juice extraction and are suitable for different preferences and juicing needs.</p></p>
