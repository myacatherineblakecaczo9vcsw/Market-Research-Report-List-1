<p><p>Multipurpose wireless inspection cameras are available in three main types based on spectrum they capture - Ultraviolet Spectrum Camera, Infrared Spectrum Camera, and Visible Spectrum Camera. Ultraviolet cameras are used for detecting flaws not visible to the human eye, such as leaks in plumbing. Infrared cameras are used for thermal imaging and detecting temperature variations. Visible spectrum cameras capture images just like normal cameras, making them versatile for various applications. Each type offers unique capabilities for different inspection needs.</p></p>
