<p><p>COVID-19 diagnostic tests market consists of different types such as Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results by detecting viral proteins in respiratory samples. ELISA tests detect antibodies in blood samples, indicating past infection. Neutralization assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit virus infection in cells. Each type of test plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring the spread of COVID-19.</p></p>
