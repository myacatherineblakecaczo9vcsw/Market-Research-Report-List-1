<p><p>Cosmetics and Personal Care Emulsifier market types include oil-in-water (o/w) emulsifiers and water-in-oil (w/o) emulsifiers. Oil-in-water emulsifiers disperse oil droplets in water, making it suitable for products like lotions and creams. Water-in-oil emulsifiers disperse water droplets in oil, suitable for products like moisturizing creams and sunscreens. Each type offers unique benefits and properties for formulating a wide range of cosmetic and personal care products, catering to different skin types and preferences.</p></p>
