<p><strong>The Oral Controlled Release Drug Delivery Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diffusion Controlled Release System</li><li>Dissolution Controlled Release System</li><li>Osmotically Controlled Release System</li><li>Dissolution & Diffusion Controlled Release System</li><li>Ion Exchange Resins Controlled Release</li><li>Hydro-dynamically Balanced Drug Delivery System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral controlled release drug delivery technology refers to a method of delivering medication that releases the drug in a controlled manner over a sustained period of time after administration. Various types of oral controlled release systems are available in the market. Diffusion controlled release systems gradually release the drug through diffusion. Dissolution controlled release systems release the drug by dissolving the drug matrix. Osmotically controlled release systems use osmotic pressure to release the drug. Dissolution & diffusion controlled release systems combine both mechanisms. Ion exchange resins controlled release involves drug release by ion exchange in the GI tract. Hydro-dynamically balanced drug delivery systems offer drug release independent of pH and gastrointestinal motility. Other types of controlled release systems also exist.</p></p>
