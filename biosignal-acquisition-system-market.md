<p><p>A biosignal acquisition system is a medical device used to capture and record physiological signals from the human body. These signals can include electrocardiogram (ECG), electromyogram (EMG), electroencephalogram (EEG), blood pressure, temperature, and various other biosignals. The system consists of sensors or electrodes that are placed on the body to detect the signals, amplifiers that enhance the signals, and data acquisition units that capture and process the signals for further analysis.</p><p>The biosignal acquisition system market is witnessing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of chronic diseases and an increasing geriatric population. These factors have led to an increased demand for monitoring and diagnosing various physiological conditions. Furthermore, technological advancements in biosignal acquisition systems, such as wireless and wearable devices, have also contributed to the market growth.</p><p>In addition, the COVID-19 pandemic has further accelerated the adoption of biosignal acquisition systems.
