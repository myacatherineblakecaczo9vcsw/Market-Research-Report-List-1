O-Type Rotary dumpers rotate the railcar upside down to unload material.</p><p>2. C-Type Rotary dumpers rotate the railcar laterally to unload material.</p><p>3. Each type has its advantages depending on the material being unloaded and the specific requirements of the industry.</p></p>
