<p><strong>The Military Platform Electro-Optics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multispectral Electro-Optics</li><li>Hyperspectral Electro-Optics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Military Platform Electro-Optics market is divided into two types: Multispectral Electro-Optics and Hyperspectral Electro-Optics. Multispectral Electro-Optics involve the use of sensors that operate in multiple spectral bands, allowing for better target detection and identification. On the other hand, Hyperspectral Electro-Optics use sensors that operate in hundreds of narrow contiguous spectral bands, providing even greater detail and capabilities for target detection and analysis. Both types of electro-optics play a crucial role in enhancing military platform capabilities and overall situational awareness.</p></p>
