<p><p>General Purpose AC contactors are categorized based on Full Load Amps (FLA) ratings. The market types for FLA Below 30A, FLA 30A-60A, FLA 60A-90A, FLA 90A-150A, and FLA Above 150A cater to different ampere requirements of electrical systems. FLA Below 30A contactors are suitable for smaller electrical loads, while FLA 30A-60A are used for moderate loads. FLA 60A-90A contactors are suitable for medium-sized loads, and FLA 90A-150A are used for heavier loads. FLA Above 150A contactors are designed for high ampere requirements in industrial applications.</p></p>
