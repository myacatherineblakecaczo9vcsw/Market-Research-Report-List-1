<p><p>Application Platform as a Service (aPaaS) is a cloud-based platform that enables the development, deployment, and management of applications without the complexity of building and maintaining the underlying infrastructure. One of the key trends is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities into aPaaS platforms. Another trend is the focus on low-code or no-code development platforms, which empower citizen developers with limited coding skills to build applications easily.</p><p>Furthermore, the aPaaS market is witnessing the emergence of multi-cloud and hybrid cloud deployment models.
