<p><p>Hospitality property management tools can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based tools operate on a remote server and are accessed through the internet, allowing users to conveniently manage their property from anywhere and on any device with an internet connection. On-premise tools, on the other hand, are installed and run locally on the user's own servers, providing direct control and customization but requiring physical presence at the property for management tasks. Both types offer their own benefits and businesses can choose the one that best suits their needs and preferences.</p></p>
