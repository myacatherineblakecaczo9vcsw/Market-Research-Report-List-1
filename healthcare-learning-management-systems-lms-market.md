<p><p>Healthcare Learning Management Systems (LMS) are software platforms designed to manage and deliver healthcare training and education. There are two market types for LMS: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based LMS is a web-based system where the software and data are stored on the provider's servers and accessed via the internet. On-premises LMS, on the other hand, is installed and operated locally on the healthcare organization's own servers. Both types offer benefits such as easy access, scalability, and customization options, but differ in terms of control, security, and maintenance responsibilities.</p></p>
