<p><p>The Alexandrite Rod market can be classified into two main types based on manufacturing methods: Czochralski Method and Float-Zone Method. The Czochralski Method involves growing single crystal rods from a molten mixture using a rotating seed crystal, resulting in high-quality Alexandrite Rods. On the other hand, the Float-Zone Method involves floating a molten zone through a cylindrical rod to produce Alexandrite Rods. Each method has its own advantages and applications, catering to different customer needs in the Alexandrite Rod market.</p></p>
