<p><p>Vacuum valves for semiconductor equipment are essential components that play a crucial role in maintaining air-tight conditions for optimal performance. Isolation valves are used to completely shut off the flow within a system, providing a reliable seal. Pressure control valves, on the other hand, regulate the pressure within the system to ensure consistency and stability. Other types of vacuum valves include gate valves, butterfly valves, and ball valves, each serving specific functions in semiconductor equipment to maintain proper vacuum levels and ensure efficient operation.</p></p>
