<p><p>Food flexible packaging market includes three main types: flexible plastic, flexible paper, and flexible foil. </p><p>Flexible plastic packaging is popular for its durability, flexibility, and ability to be molded into various shapes. It is widely used for packaging snacks, candies, and ready-to-eat meals. </p><p>Flexible paper packaging is lightweight, eco-friendly, and recyclable. It is commonly used for packaging dry foods like cereals, pasta, and bakery products.</p><p>Flexible foil packaging provides excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light, making it ideal for packaging perishable foods like meats, cheeses, and coffee.</p></p>
