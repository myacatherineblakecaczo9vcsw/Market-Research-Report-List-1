<p><p>Recycled fiber fabric for apparel comes in two main market types: cellulose system and protein system. Cellulose system fabrics are made from recycled plant-based fibers like cotton and bamboo, offering a sustainable and breathable option for clothing. Protein system fabrics, on the other hand, are made from recycled animal-based fibers like wool and silk, providing warmth and luxury feel for clothing. Both market types contribute to sustainable fashion by utilizing recycled materials in the production of apparel.</p></p>
