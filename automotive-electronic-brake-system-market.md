<p><p>The Automotive Electronic Brake System market comprises three main types: Traction Control System (TCS), which helps prevent wheel spin during acceleration; Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), which prevents the wheels from locking up during braking; and Electronic Stability Control (ESC), which helps maintain vehicle stability and prevent skidding. These systems work together to enhance safety and control while driving, by quickly adjusting brake pressure and engine power to ensure optimal traction and stability on the road.</p></p>
