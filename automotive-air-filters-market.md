There are two main types of automotive air filters - the intake air filter and the cabin air filter. The intake air filter is primarily located in the engine compartment and is responsible for filtering the air that enters the engine, while the cabin air filter is located inside the vehicle and is responsible for filtering the air that enters the passenger cabin. Both filters play a crucial role in ensuring clean and efficient air circulation within the vehicle.</p></p>
