<p><p>Li-ion Battery Separator Market Types include:</p><p>1. Weaving Membrane: A type of separator made by weaving fibers together to create a porous structure for ion transport.</p><p>2. Nonwoven Membrane: Consists of randomly arranged fibers bonded together to form a porous network for ion movement.</p><p>3. Microporous Membrane: Features tiny pores that allow ions to pass through while blocking larger particles.</p><p>4. Composite Membrane: A combination of different materials to achieve desired properties for ion conductivity.</p><p>5. Other: Includes specialty separators with unique designs or functionalities not classified in the above categories.</p></p>
