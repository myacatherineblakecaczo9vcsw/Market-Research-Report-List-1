<p><p>Plasma etching agents are chemicals used in the semiconductor industry to remove material from a surface through either wet or dry etching processes. Wet etching agents involve immersing the material in a liquid solution to dissolve unwanted material, while dry etching agents use plasma to bombard the material with ions or radicals to remove material. Both types of etching agents have specific applications and advantages in various industries, with dry etching agents being preferred for their precision and efficiency in high-tech manufacturing processes.</p></p>
