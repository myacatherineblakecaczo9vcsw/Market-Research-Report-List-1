<p><p>Hybrid security paper includes various features to enhance its counterfeit protection. Substrates refer to the base material of the paper, which can have unique characteristics for authentication. Watermarks are designs or text embedded into the paper during manufacturing, visible when held up to light. Threads are embedded within the paper and can include optical features like microtext or color-shifting ink. Holograms are three-dimensional images that can be embedded on the paper for added security. All these elements work together to create a multifaceted security solution for documents and currency.</p></p>
