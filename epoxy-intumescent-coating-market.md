<p><ul><li>General Type</li><li>Cryogenic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General Type epoxy intumescent coating market comprises coatings that are designed for fire protection in normal temperature conditions. These coatings expand and form a protective char layer when exposed to heat, preventing the spread of fire. Cryogenic Type epoxy intumescent coatings are specifically formulated for use in extremely low temperatures, such as in cryogenic storage tanks. These coatings offer fire protection while also maintaining flexibility and adhesion in freezing conditions. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring fire safety in various environments.</p></p>
