<p><p>Single Port and Dual Port are types of Electronic Intelligent Tourniquets that are used in medical settings to control and monitor blood flow during surgeries or medical procedures. Single Port tourniquets have one port for inflation and deflation, while Dual Port tourniquets have two separate ports for inflation and deflation. Dual Port tourniquets offer more precise control over pressure levels and can be easier to use in certain situations, while Single Port tourniquets are more straightforward and easier to operate for basic applications.</p></p>
