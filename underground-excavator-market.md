<p><p>The underground excavator market consists of three main types: diesel, electric, and hydraulic excavators. Diesel underground excavators are powered by diesel engines, providing high power and efficiency for heavy-duty excavation tasks. Electric underground excavators use electric motors for operation, offering lower emissions and quieter operation. Hydraulic underground excavators rely on hydraulic systems to power the digging arm and bucket, providing precise control and versatility in various underground construction applications. Each type of excavator caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
