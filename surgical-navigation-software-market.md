<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (EM)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical navigation software market includes three main types: optical, electromagnetic (EM), and hybrid. Optical navigation systems use cameras and infrared markers to track surgical instruments in real-time. Electromagnetic systems employ sensors to detect the position and orientation of instruments through electromagnetic fields. Hybrid systems combine optical and electromagnetic technologies for enhanced accuracy and flexibility during surgical procedures. These different types of navigation software play a crucial role in guiding surgeons, providing precision, improving patient safety, and aiding in complex surgeries.</p></p>
