<p><p>Single bird cage market types refer to the different levels or decks in which birds are housed. Single-deck bird cages are the most common type, with individual cages stacked one on top of the other. Double-deck bird cages consist of two levels of cages stacked together, providing more room for birds to move around. Triple-deck bird cages have three levels of cages stacked on top of one another, allowing for even more birds to be housed in a limited space. Each type offers a different configuration and capacity for bird owners to choose from.</p></p>
