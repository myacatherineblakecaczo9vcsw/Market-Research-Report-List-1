<p><strong>The PTFE Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE membranes are divided into different types based on their properties and applications. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water, making them ideal for applications where water needs to be blocked. On the other hand, hydrophilic PTFE membranes attract water, making them suitable for filtration and separation processes. Other types of PTFE membranes include specialty membranes designed for specific applications, such as pore size control or chemical resistance. Each type of PTFE membrane offers unique properties and advantages for various industrial and scientific purposes.</p></p>
