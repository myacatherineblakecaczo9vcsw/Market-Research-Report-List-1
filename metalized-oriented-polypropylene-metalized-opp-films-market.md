<p><p>Metalized Oriented Polypropylene (Metalized OPP) Films are categorized based on their thickness into four market types. Films below 10 microns are light and flexible, suitable for applications like packaging of food and pharmaceutical products. Films between 10-30 microns provide a balance of strength and flexibility, making them ideal for laminating and printing purposes. Films in the 30-60 micron range offer enhanced barrier properties, making them suitable for packaging items that require protection against moisture and light. Films above 60 microns are thick and durable, commonly used in industrial applications such as insulation and construction.</p></p>
