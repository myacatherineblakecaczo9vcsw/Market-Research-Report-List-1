<p><p>The Full Glass Curtain Wall Market consists of three main types: Single Glazed Type, Double Glazed Type, and Three Glazed Type. Single Glazed Type involves a single layer of glass, providing basic insulation and minimal soundproofing. Double Glazed Type features two layers of glass with a sealed air space in between, offering improved insulation and sound reduction. Three Glazed Type includes three layers of glass with two air spaces, providing the highest level of insulation and soundproofing. These different types cater to varying needs for building design and energy efficiency.</p></p>
