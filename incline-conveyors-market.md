<p><p>Incline conveyors can be classified based on the type of belt they use. One classification is based on flat belt conveyors, which utilize a continuous flat belt to transport materials up an incline. Another classification is based on modular belt conveyors, which use individual interlocking modules to create a belt that can navigate inclines and declines. Both types of incline conveyors are used in various industries for efficiently moving materials in a vertical direction.</p></p>
