<p><p>Cold food wells are divided into two main market types: non-refrigerated and refrigerated. Non-refrigerated cold food wells are commonly used for displaying items like fruits, pastries, or bread that do not require refrigeration. These wells typically rely on ice or gel packs to keep items cool. In contrast, refrigerated cold food wells are used for items that need to be kept at a certain temperature, such as deli meats, salads, or dairy products. These wells have built-in refrigeration units to maintain the desired temperature for food safety.</p></p>
