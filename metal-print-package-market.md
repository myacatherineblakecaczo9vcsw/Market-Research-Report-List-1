<p><p>The metal print package market offers various printing techniques to create stunning packaging designs. Offset lithography is a popular method that transfers ink from a metal plate to a rubber blanket, resulting in high-quality prints. Gravure utilizes engraved cylinders to apply ink onto the package, ensuring precise and consistent imagery. Flexography employs flexible rubber plates for printing on uneven surfaces like metal. Digital printing uses advanced technology for quick and customizable prints, while "Other" refers to emerging techniques or a combination of multiple methods. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences within the metal print package industry.</p></p>
