<p><p>Leaching agitation tanks are commonly used in the mining industry for extracting valuable minerals from ores. There are two main types of leaching agitation tanks: air agitation tanks and mechanical agitation tanks. Air agitation tanks use compressed air to create agitation, which helps in the mixing and settling of particles in the tank. On the other hand, mechanical agitation tanks use mechanical systems, such as impellers or paddles, to create agitation. Both types have their advantages and are chosen based on specific requirements and the nature of the materials being processed.</p></p>
