<p><p>The JFET transistor market consists of two main types: N-channel JFET Transistor and P-channel JFET Transistor. N-channel JFET transistors are designed to conduct current between the source and drain terminals when a positive voltage is applied to the gate terminal. On the other hand, P-channel JFET transistors conduct current when a negative voltage is applied to the gate terminal. These transistors are widely used in a variety of electronic devices and circuits to control and amplify electrical signals.</p></p>
