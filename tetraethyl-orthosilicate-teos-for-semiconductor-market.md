<p><p>Tetraethyl orthosilicate (TEOS) is a common precursor used in the semiconductor industry for various applications such as chemical vapor deposition (CVD), atomic layer deposition (ALD), and trench filling. In CVD, TEOS is used to deposit thin films of silicon dioxide onto semiconductor substrates. In ALD, it is used to form thin, conformal films with precise control over thickness. In trench filling, TEOS is used to fill narrow trenches with silicon dioxide to provide insulation and planarization for the semiconductor device.</p></p>
