<p><p>Hydraulic tubing market consists of two main types: Spiral Wire Hydraulic Tubing and Wire Braided Hydraulic Tubing. Spiral Wire Hydraulic Tubing is constructed with multiple layers of spiral wire reinforcement for high pressure applications, offering strong resistance to bending and crushing. On the other hand, Wire Braided Hydraulic Tubing features a single or double layer of braided wire for medium pressure applications, providing flexibility and ease of installation. Both types of tubing are essential components in hydraulic systems for transmitting fluid under pressure.</p></p>
