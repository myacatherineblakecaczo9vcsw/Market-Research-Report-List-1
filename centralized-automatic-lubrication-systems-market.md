<p><p>Centralized Automatic Lubrication Systems are divided into two main types: Grease Lubrication System and Oil Lubrication System. </p><p>Grease Lubrication Systems use grease as the lubricating agent, which is pumped through a series of lines to lubricate various machine components. </p><p>Oil Lubrication Systems, on the other hand, use oil as the lubricating agent. These systems typically involve a reservoir of oil that is pumped through a network of pipes to deliver lubrication to the necessary parts of machinery. Both systems help ensure optimal performance and longevity of industrial equipment.</p></p>
