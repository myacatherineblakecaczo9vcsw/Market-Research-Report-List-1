<p><p>The Pulmonary Dilation Balloon market consists of two main types: Elation™ and CRE™. Elation™ is a type of balloon used for the dilation of the pulmonary artery during a medical procedure. It helps to improve blood flow and relieve blockages in the artery. On the other hand, CRE™ is another type of dilation balloon specifically designed for use in the coronary artery during angioplasty procedures. It helps to widen the artery and restore proper blood flow to the heart muscles. Both types of balloons are essential tools in treating various cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
