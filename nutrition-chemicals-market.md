This has led to a rise in demand for nutritionally fortified food products. Secondly, there is a growing aging population, resulting in a higher demand for nutritionally enhanced products that can cater to specific health conditions of the elderly. Lastly, the rising prevalence of chronic diseases and the need for preventive healthcare have also contributed to the growth of this market.</p><p>One of the latest trends observed in the nutrition chemicals market is the increasing popularity of organic and natural food products.
