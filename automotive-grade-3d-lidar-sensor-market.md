<p><p>Automotive Grade 3D LiDAR sensors come in two main types - Mechanical LiDAR and Solid State LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR systems use moving parts to scan the environment, offering high resolution and long-range capabilities. On the other hand, Solid State LiDAR systems are compact and durable, utilizing solid-state technology to provide reliable and precise detection without any moving parts. Both types of LiDAR sensors play a crucial role in autonomous vehicles and advanced driver assistance systems in the automotive industry.</p></p>
