<p><p>Probiotic ingredients for animal use typically come in three main types: bacteria, yeast, and spore formers. Bacteria-based probiotics are the most commonly used and include strains like lactobacillus and bifidobacterium. Yeast-based probiotics, such as saccharomyces cerevisiae, are also popular and offer unique benefits. Spore formers, like bacillus subtilis, are known for their ability to survive harsh conditions in the digestive tract. Each type of probiotic ingredient offers distinct advantages and can be used to promote gut health and overall well-being in animals.</p></p>
