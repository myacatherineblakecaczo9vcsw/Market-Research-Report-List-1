<p><p>Oil field chemicals market can be divided into different types such as alkyl phenols and ethoxylated aps, glutaraldehyde-based biocides, polyacrylamide, and pour point depressants. Alkyl phenols and ethoxylated aps are used as surfactants for enhanced oil recovery. Glutaraldehyde-based biocides are used for microbial control in oil wells. Polyacrylamide is used for viscosity control and as a friction reducer. Pour point depressants are used to lower the temperature at which crude oil becomes too viscous to flow efficiently.</p></p>
