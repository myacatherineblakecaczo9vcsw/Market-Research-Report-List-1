<p><p>Mechanical sweepers are categorized into two main types: 4-wheel sweepers and 3-wheel sweepers. 4-wheel sweepers are robust machines that are typically larger in size and have a higher capacity for collecting debris. These sweepers are commonly used in larger areas such as parking lots, streets, and industrial sites. On the other hand, 3-wheel sweepers are more compact and agile, making them suitable for smaller spaces like sidewalks, pathways, and narrow streets. Both types of sweepers are essential for maintaining cleanliness in various environments.</p></p>
