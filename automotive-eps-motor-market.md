<p><p>Automotive EPS motors are divided into four main types: CEPS Motor, REPS Motor, PEPS Motor, and EHPS Motor. CEPS Motor refers to column-type EPS motors, REPS Motor stands for rack-assist EPS motors, PEPS Motor is power-assist EPS motors, and EHPS Motor refers to electro-hydraulic power steering motors. Each type of motor functions in a specific way to assist in steering control in different automotive vehicles. These motors play a crucial role in enhancing the overall driving experience and safety of vehicles on the road.</p></p>
