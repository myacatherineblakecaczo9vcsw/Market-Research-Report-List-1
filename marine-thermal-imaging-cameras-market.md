<p><strong>The Marine Thermal Imaging Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine thermal imaging cameras come in two main types: uncooled and cooled. Uncooled cameras use microbolometers to detect infrared radiation without the need for cooling systems, making them more compact, cost-effective, and suitable for short-range applications. Cooled cameras, on the other hand, use cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and image clarity, making them ideal for long-range surveillance and tracking targets in challenging conditions. Both types have their advantages and are used in various marine applications based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
