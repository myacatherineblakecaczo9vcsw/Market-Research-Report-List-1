<p><p>Industrial handwheels are available in various types to suit different applications. Spoked handwheels feature a wheel with spokes for easy gripping and turning. Solid handwheels are a single piece design without any spokes, providing a more sturdy and durable option. Arm handwheels have an extended arm attached to the wheel, offering increased leverage for turning. Each type of handwheel serves a specific function based on the requirements of the industrial machinery or equipment it is used with.</p></p>
