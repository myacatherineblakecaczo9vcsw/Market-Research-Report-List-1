<p><p>Off-the-road tires are classified into three main types based on their construction and material - pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires consist of an inner tube filled with compressed air, providing a cushioning effect for rough terrain. Solid tires are made of solid rubber, eliminating the risk of punctures and requiring less maintenance. Polyurethane tires are lightweight and durable, suitable for use in certain industrial applications. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the equipment and environment.</p></p>
