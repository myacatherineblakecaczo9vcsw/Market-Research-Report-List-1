<p><strong>The Anti-fouling Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-fouling coating market is divided into two types - Type I and Type II based on their composition and performance. Type I coatings are typically solvent-based and offer good protection against fouling organisms, such as algae and barnacles. On the other hand, Type II coatings are water-based and provide superior protection against a wider range of organisms, including slime and bacteria. Type II coatings are often more environmentally friendly compared to Type I coatings. Both types of coatings are essential for protecting marine equipment and structures from biofouling.</p></p>
