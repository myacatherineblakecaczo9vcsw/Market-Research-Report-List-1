<p><ul><li>Gel</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester matting agents are available in two main types - gel and powder. Gel matting agents are typically used in liquid coatings to provide a matte finish by forming a layer on the surface to scatter and absorb light. Powder matting agents are solid particles that are dispersed in the coating to create a matte appearance. Both types of matting agents are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, furniture, and packaging to achieve different levels of gloss and texture in coatings.</p></p>
