<p><ul><li>Anticholinergic</li><li>Antihistamines</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seasickness medicine market can be categorized into three types - Anticholinergic, Antihistamines, and Others. Anticholinergic medicines work by blocking the action of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that plays a role in triggering seasickness symptoms. Antihistamines, on the other hand, work by inhibiting the effect of histamine, a substance released during motion sickness. The "Others" category includes alternative therapies and natural remedies that provide relief from seasickness symptoms, such as acupressure bands or ginger-based products. These medications are commonly used to alleviate the uncomfortable symptoms of seasickness.</p></p>
