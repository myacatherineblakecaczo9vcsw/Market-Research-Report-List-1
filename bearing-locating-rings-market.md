<p><p>Bearing locating rings are essential components used in machinery to properly position and support bearings. AN rings, BN rings, and ANL rings are different types of bearing locating rings. AN rings are designed for cylindrical roller bearings, BN rings are used for tapered roller bearings, and ANL rings are suitable for needle roller bearings. Each type of ring is specifically engineered to ensure the correct alignment and fit of different bearing types, ultimately contributing to the efficient operation and longevity of machinery.</p></p>
