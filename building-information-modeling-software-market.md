<p><p>Building Information Modeling (BIM) software refers to technology used in the architecture, engineering, and construction industries. BIM software can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based BIM software is operated and stored on remote servers, allowing users to access and collaborate on projects online. It offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. On-premise BIM software, on the other hand, is installed and operated on local computers or servers. It provides greater control over data security but may require more upfront investment and maintenance. Both types enable professionals to create, manage, and share digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of a building project.</p></p>
