<p><p>Cable protection in the automotive industry comprises three main market types: abrasion protection, heat protection, and impact protection. Abrasion protection guards against damage caused by friction, such as rubbing against sharp edges or rough surfaces. Heat protection prevents cables from potential heat sources, ensuring optimal performance and preventing overheating. Impact protection shields cables from external forces, reducing the risk of damage from collisions or excessive vibrations. These market types collectively aim to maintain the durability, efficiency, and safety of automotive cables.</p></p>
