<p><p>RPA technology market can be categorized into two types: desktop and server-based. Desktop RPA technology refers to software bots that are installed and operate on individual users' computers. These bots automate repetitive tasks and workflows on the desktop environment. On the other hand, server-based RPA technology involves software bots that are deployed and run on servers or the cloud. These bots can automate complex and high-volume tasks across multiple systems and applications, offering scalability and centralized management capabilities. Both types of RPA technology are designed to enhance efficiency and productivity by reducing manual effort and human errors.</p></p>
