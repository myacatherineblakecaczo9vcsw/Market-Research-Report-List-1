<p><p>Customer Experience Management (CEM) is a practice that revolves around improving customer satisfaction and loyalty. The market for CEM can be classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CEM solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing businesses to access them via the internet. They offer flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On-premise CEM solutions, on the other hand, are installed and maintained within a company's infrastructure. They provide more control and customization but require higher initial investments. Both types aim to enhance customer experiences but differ in their deployment and management models.</p></p>
