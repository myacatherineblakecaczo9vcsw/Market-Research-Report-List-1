<p><p>Automatic pharmaceutical packaging machines are categorized into three main types based on their functions in the packaging process. Primary packaging machines are used to directly package the pharmaceutical products, ensuring their safety and integrity. Secondary packaging machines handle the packaging of primary packages into larger units for distribution. Labeling and serialization machines are responsible for affixing labels to the packages and ensuring that each product is uniquely identified and tracked. These machines play crucial roles in the efficiency and safety of pharmaceutical packaging processes.</p></p>
