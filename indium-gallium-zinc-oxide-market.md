<p><p>Indium Gallium Zinc Oxide (IGZO) is a semiconductor material used in various electronic devices. There are two main types of IGZO markets: Crystalline IGZO and Amorphous IGZO. Crystalline IGZO is characterized by a well-ordered atomic structure, providing high electron mobility and better performance in applications requiring high speed and resolution. On the other hand, Amorphous IGZO has a disordered atomic structure, making it more flexible and easier to manufacture on large surfaces, making it suitable for applications like flexible displays and sensors.</p></p>
