<p><strong>The Air Cycle Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Cycle</li><li>Two-wheel Bootstrap</li><li>Three-wheel</li><li>Four-wheel/Dual-spool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air cycle machines can be categorized into different market types based on the number of wheels or spools they have. Simple cycle machines have a single wheel for compressing and expanding air. Two-wheel bootstrap machines have two wheels connected in series. Three-wheel machines have an additional turbine wheel for further expansion. Four-wheel or dual-spool machines have two separate compressor and turbine spools, providing more efficient performance and power output. Each market type offers varying levels of efficiency and performance based on their design.</p></p>
