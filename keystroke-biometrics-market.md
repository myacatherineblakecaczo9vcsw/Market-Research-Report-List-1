<p><strong>The Keystroke Biometrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Keystroke Biometric</li><li>Dynamic Keystroke Biometrics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Keystroke biometrics refers to the analysis of an individual's typing patterns as a unique identifier. The market can be categorized into two types: static and dynamic keystroke biometrics. Static keystroke biometric focuses on the individual's fixed typing pattern, such as the time interval between keystrokes. Dynamic keystroke biometric, on the other hand, considers the variation in typing patterns within a session, including the pressure applied to the keys. Both types aim to authenticate users based on their distinct typing behavior, providing secure and efficient identity verification solutions.</p></p>
