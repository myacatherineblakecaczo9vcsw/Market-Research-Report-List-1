<p><p>Printed signage can be classified into two main types - Indoor and Outdoor. Indoor printed signage is designed for use inside buildings, such as offices, retail stores, and restaurants. It includes items like posters, banners, and wall decals. Outdoor printed signage, on the other hand, is intended for outdoor use and is often used for promotional purposes like advertising a business or event. This includes items like billboards, storefront signs, and vehicle wraps. Both types play a crucial role in marketing and communication efforts.</p></p>
