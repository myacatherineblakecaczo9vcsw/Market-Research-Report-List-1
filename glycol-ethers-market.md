<p><p>Glycol Ethers are classified into two main types: Propylene oxide-based (P-series) and Ethylene oxide-based (E-series). The P-series glycol ethers are derived from propylene oxide and are commonly used as solvents in a variety of industries. On the other hand, the E-series glycol ethers are derived from ethylene oxide and are primarily used as solvents and coalescing agents in various applications. Both types play a crucial role in industries such as paints, coatings, cleaners, and automotive products.</p></p>
