<p><p>The Metal Noise Barrier Market can be divided into two main types: Ground-Mounted Noise Barrier and Structure-Mounted Noise Barrier. Ground-Mounted Noise Barriers are installed directly on the ground and are used to mitigate noise pollution from traffic, construction sites, or industrial facilities. On the other hand, Structure-Mounted Noise Barriers are attached to existing structures such as bridges, walls, or buildings to reduce noise levels. Both types of noise barriers play a crucial role in creating quieter and more peaceful urban environments.</p></p>
