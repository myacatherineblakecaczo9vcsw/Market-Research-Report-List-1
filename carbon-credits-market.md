One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing focus on sustainable development and the need to combat climate change. Lastly, the growing involvement of the financial sector, including banks and investment funds, in carbon trading is also driving market growth.</p><p>The latest trends in the carbon credits market include the emergence of new types of carbon credits, such as nature-based or biodiversity credits, which are generated by protecting or restoring natural ecosystems.
