<p><strong>The Direct Burial Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct burial cables are used for underground installations and come in two main types: solid and twisted. Solid direct burial cables have a single, solid copper conductor which provides durability and stability in harsh environments. Twisted direct burial cables have multiple insulated conductors twisted together, providing better signal transmission and resistance to interference. Both types are commonly used in outdoor applications such as in-ground lighting, landscape irrigation systems, and underground telecommunications installations.</p></p>
