<p><p>Primary Care EHR Market Types refer to the different ways in which Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems are implemented and accessed. Cloud-Based EHR systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, allowing healthcare providers to store and access patient data securely from any location. On-Premise EHR systems, on the other hand, are installed and maintained locally on a healthcare organization's own servers. This provides greater control and customization options, but requires in-house IT infrastructure and expertise. Both market types offer their own benefits and considerations for primary care providers.</p></p>
