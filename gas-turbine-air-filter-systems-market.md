<p><strong>The Gas Turbine Air Filter Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barrier (Static) Filters</li><li>Self-Cleaning Pulse Filters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Barrier (static) filters are commonly used in gas turbine air filter systems to remove particulate matter from the air stream. These filters rely on a physical barrier to trap particles as the air passes through. Self-cleaning pulse filters use a pulse of compressed air to dislodge and remove particles from the filter, allowing for continuous use without manual cleaning. Other types of filters, such as pre-filters and HEPA filters, may also be used in gas turbine air filtration systems to achieve specific filtration requirements.</p></p>
