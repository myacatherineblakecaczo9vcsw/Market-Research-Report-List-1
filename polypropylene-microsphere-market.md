<p><p>Polypropylene microspheres are used in a variety of applications, with the two main types being hollow and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are lightweight and provide insulation and buoyancy properties, making them ideal for use in composites and syntactic foams. Solid microspheres are denser and offer strength and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring high impact resistance. Both types of microspheres are used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, construction, and cosmetics for their unique properties and advantages.</p></p>
