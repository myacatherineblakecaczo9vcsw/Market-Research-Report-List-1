<p><p>Solvent-Thinned Coatings for Exterior Wall: These coatings are solvent-based and are suitable for exterior walls where a durable and weather-resistant finish is required. They are inorganic, which means they do not contain any volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and are more environmentally friendly. These coatings are suitable for high-traffic areas and provide excellent protection against harsh weather conditions.</p></p>
