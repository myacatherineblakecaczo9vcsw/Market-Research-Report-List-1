<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Electric Turbine</li><li>Axial Flow Electric Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric turbines are categorized into two main types: centrifugal and axial flow turbines. Centrifugal electric turbines operate by using centrifugal force to propel air or gas through the turbine. On the other hand, axial flow electric turbines feature blades that rotate around a central shaft, allowing for a more streamlined flow of air or gas through the turbine. Both types of turbines are commonly used in various industrial applications for generating power or providing ventilation.</p></p>
