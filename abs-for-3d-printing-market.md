Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves the polymerization of monomers in an emulsion, resulting in a more controlled and finely-tuned polymer structure. Bulk Copolymerization, on the other hand, involves the direct polymerization of monomers in a bulk phase, offering a simpler and more cost-effective process. Other markets may include specialty formulations or customized ABS materials for specific applications in 3D printing.</p></p>
