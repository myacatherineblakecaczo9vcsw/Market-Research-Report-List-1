<p><p>Riser sleeves for casting are used to regulate the solidification process of molten metal during casting. Insulation riser sleeves help to retain heat, allowing for longer solidification times and reducing the risk of defects. Conversely, feeder riser sleeves are designed to feed additional molten metal into the casting as it cools to prevent shrinkage defects. Both types of riser sleeves play a crucial role in ensuring high-quality castings with minimal defects in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
