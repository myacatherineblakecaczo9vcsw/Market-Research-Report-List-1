<p><p>Screen inks are inks used for screen printing, a technique commonly used for printing on fabrics, signage, and packaging materials. Solvent-based printing inks use organic solvents as a medium to disperse the colorants and are known for their durability and vibrant colors. On the other hand, water-based printing inks use water as a medium to disperse the colorants and are considered more environmentally friendly and safe to use. Both types of inks have their own set of advantages and are suitable for different printing applications.</p></p>
