<p><strong>The Enterprise Search Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise search engine market can be divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based enterprise search engines are hosted on remote servers and provide search capabilities through the internet. They offer scalability and flexibility, as the infrastructure is managed by the service provider. On the other hand, on-premises enterprise search engines are installed and maintained within the organization's own infrastructure. This offers greater control and security, as the data remains within the organization's network. The choice between cloud-based and on-premises depends on factors such as data sensitivity, control requirements, and organizational preferences.</p></p>
