Printing Auxiliaries are additives used in the printing process for enhancing color and printing quality. Pretreatment Auxiliaries are chemicals used to prepare textiles for dyeing or printing. Finishing Auxiliaries are chemicals used to improve the final appearance, feel, and performance of textiles. Each type plays a crucial role in the textile industry.</p></p>
