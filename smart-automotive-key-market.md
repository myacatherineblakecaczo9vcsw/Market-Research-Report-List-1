<p><p>Passive Keyless Access systems enable drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without needing to physically remove the key from their pocket or bag. It works by detecting the key within proximity of the vehicle, automatically unlocking the doors and allowing for push-button start. Remote Keyless Access systems, on the other hand, require the driver to push a button on the key fob to unlock or lock the doors and start the engine from a distance. Both types of smart automotive keys offer convenience and improved security for drivers.</p></p>
