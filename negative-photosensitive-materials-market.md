<p><p>Negative photosensitive materials are used in various industries like electronics, printing, and packaging. There are two major types of markets for these materials: Thermal Curing and UV Curing. Thermal curing materials require heat to develop the image on the substrate, while UV curing materials use ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process. Both markets cater to different applications and offer unique advantages such as fast curing times, high resolution, and excellent adhesion properties. Overall, these materials play a crucial role in modern manufacturing processes.</p></p>
