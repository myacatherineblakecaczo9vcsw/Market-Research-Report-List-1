Multimode PM Fiber is designed for short-distance communication and is capable of transmitting multiple signals simultaneously. Single-Mode PM Fiber, on the other hand, is used for long-distance communication and is ideal for transmitting a single signal with minimal distortion. Both types of fibers offer high polarization extinction ratio and are crucial for ensuring accurate and efficient optical communication systems.</p></p>
