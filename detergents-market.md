<p><p>Detergents market can be segmented into four main types based on their chemical properties - Anionic Detergents, Cationic Detergents, Non-ionic Detergents, and Zwitterionic (Amphoteric) Detergents. Anionic detergents are negatively charged and commonly used in household cleaning products. Cationic detergents are positively charged and often found in fabric softeners. Non-ionic detergents have no charge and are gentle on fabrics. Zwitterionic detergents have both positive and negative charges, making them versatile for a wide range of cleaning applications.</p></p>
