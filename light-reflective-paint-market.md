<p><strong>The Light Reflective Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transparent</li><li>Opaque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light reflective paint can be categorized into two main types: transparent and opaque. Transparent light reflective paint allows light to pass through it, while still reflecting light and providing visibility. This type of paint is often used in applications where maintaining the aesthetics of the underlying surface is important. On the other hand, opaque light reflective paint completely blocks light from passing through, providing a solid and highly reflective surface. This type of paint is commonly used for high-visibility safety markings and signage.</p></p>
