<p><ul><li>Simple-face Tape</li><li>Double-sided Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyester adhesive tape is categorized into two main types: simple-face tape and double-sided tape. Simple-face tape has adhesive on only one side, making it suitable for joining two surfaces together. Double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, enabling it to stick two surfaces together. Simple-face tape is commonly used for packaging, sealing, and labeling, while double-sided tape is often used for bonding materials, mounting objects, and laminating. Both types of polyester adhesive tape offer strong adhesion and durability.</p></p>
