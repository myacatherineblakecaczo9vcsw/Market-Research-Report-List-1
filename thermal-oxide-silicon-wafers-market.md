<p><p>The Thermal Oxide Silicon Wafers market can be categorized into two types: Dry Thermal Oxide and Wet Thermal Oxide. Dry Thermal Oxide refers to a process where silicon wafers are exposed to high temperatures in the absence of water, resulting in the formation of a thin layer of oxide on the surface. Wet Thermal Oxide, on the other hand, involves subjecting the silicon wafers to high temperatures in the presence of water vapor to create the oxide layer. Both methods are commonly used in the semiconductor industry for various applications.</p></p>
