<p><p>Battery Grade Graphite market is segmented into three types: Crystalline Flake Graphite, Amorphous Graphite, and Vein Graphite (Lump). Crystalline Flake Graphite is highly preferred for its superior conductivity and high purity, making it ideal for lithium-ion batteries. Amorphous Graphite is commonly used in alkaline batteries due to its lower purity levels. Vein Graphite (Lump) is the rarest form, known for its high carbon content and excellent thermal conductivity, suitable for high-performance batteries. Each type caters to different battery requirements based on their unique properties.</p></p>
